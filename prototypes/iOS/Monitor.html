OpenLCB Monitor App

The OpenLCB Monitor app for iPhone connections via Ethernet to your CAN-based OpenLCB, and displays the traffic as it happens.

You can get a free copy from the Apple App Store (once available in June 2011), or check out a copy from OpenLCB and built it for yourself.

Version 0.2 of the app displays the raw messages, along with limited decoding of the format and addresses. We're working on the next version, which will do complete decoding of OpenLCB version 0.3.
